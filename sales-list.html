Sales List

The ‘Sales List’ page will allow you to view all Copart Middle East vehicle inventory for upcoming auctions. Copart UAE, Oman and Bahrain all have separate sales Lists that are created for every auction. Copart MEA buyers can differentiate each yard sales list by viewing the yard number, name, date and time mentioned on the Sales List.
