Copart GCC Member Protection Pledge (MPP) Program Terms and Conditions The Copart MPP is offered on purchases of any item listed on www.copartmea.com. Under the MPP, Copart pledges that for vehicles that are purchased for less than AED 75,000:

  1. The year, make, and model are correct as listed in Copart’s vehicle description
  2. The vehicle images represent the general appearance of the exterior and interior of the vehicle at the time of receipt of the vehicle by Copart
  3. The odometer reading for vehicles is accurate, where provided
  4. Valid legal ownership will be passed to you
  5. The ownership documents you receive will match the Title /Type description listed on www.copartmea.com
  6. If you pay Copart you are guaranteed to receive the vehicle
  7. You will be able to pay for and retrieve any vehicle purchased by you during Copart’s regular business hours up to a half hour prior to close
  8. The vehicle’s running condition has been accurately classified according to criteria for those programs as at the date of receipt of the vehicle
